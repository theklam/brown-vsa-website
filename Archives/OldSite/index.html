Chào các bạn!
Hello my friends!

Since 1986, the Brown Vietnamese Students Association has sought to provide a space for Vietnamese people, culture, society, history, and politics. Throughout the year we host events such as retreats, panels, workshops, dinners/banquets, film screenings, fashion shows to name a few. We hope to create a safe, open, and honest community where we can watch movies, plan events, eat pho, and catch up with one another! We are pumped for our events this semester, so feel free to join VSA!

There is always a place for you at this table and we hope you’ll join us at our weekly dinners or various events. Looking forward to meeting you!

Your co-presidents,
Brandon and Arthur
