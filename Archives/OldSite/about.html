Mission

The mission of the VSA is to promote the understanding and appreciation of the cultural, social, political, historical, and identity of people of Vietnamese heritage by providing a safe space for discussion, exploration, and community building. Our activities include celebrating Vietnamese holidays, discussing current issues that are pertinent to Vietnamese and Vietnamese Americans, and participating in events that connect us to Vietnamese culture and traditions.
